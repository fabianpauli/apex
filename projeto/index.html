SERVICE OF EACH STAGE OF THE SUPPLY CHAIN

CHEP helps customers move, store and protect materials as they flow through each stage of their supply chain.

Source

Many of our customers around the world are using our pallets, containers and crates to manage the flow of raw materials and other inputs they source from suppliers for their processing & production facilities.

Maker

We have a lot of food and non-food manufacturing customers across a range of industries using timber pallets, plastic pallets, intermediate bulk containers and container accessories in their production facilities.

Deliver

Whether you are a vegetable grower, a meat packer, a breakfast cereal or paint manufacturer - companies of all types operating supply chains want their product to reach their customers in good condition, in the most cost effective way.
